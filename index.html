Privacy Policy for Poker Puzzles

Effective Date: 1/8/2026

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and disclosed when you use the Poker Puzzles mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Poker Puzzles does not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

However, the app uses third-party services that may collect certain data automatically, including:

  • Advertising Data: The app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as Apple’s IDFA), approximate location (e.g., country or region), ad interaction data, and other technical information to provide and improve ads.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with ads (such as impressions and clicks).
  • Diagnostics: Crash logs and performance data related to app stability and ad delivery.

This data may be associated with a device identifier and is handled by Google in accordance with their privacy policies.

Poker Puzzles itself stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Game Progress & High Scores: Stored locally on your device and not transmitted to our servers.

2. How We Use Information

Information collected through the app is used for the following purposes:

  • To display advertisements and support the app
  • To understand ad performance and improve user experience
  • To monitor app performance and diagnose crashes
  • To track in-game progress and high scores locally on your device

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information directly. However, third-party services such as Google AdMob may collect and process data as described above. Their use of information is governed by their own privacy policies.

You can learn more about Google’s privacy practices here:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Retention

Poker Puzzles does not store personal data on its own servers. Gameplay data such as high scores is stored locally on your device. Data collected by third-party services is retained according to their respective privacy policies.

5. Your Choices

Depending on your device settings, you may be able to limit ad tracking by adjusting your privacy settings on your device. You may also control whether personalized ads are shown through system-level options.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.
